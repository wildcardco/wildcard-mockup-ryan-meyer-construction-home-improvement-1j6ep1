Who you're hiring

A local crew, not a lead-gen funnel

Meyer Construction Home Improvement is based right on South Indiana Avenue in Crown Point. When you call, you're not routed to a sales floor three states away — you're talking to the people who will measure your openings, order your windows, and swing the hammer.

We work mostly on established and older homes around Lake County: the ones with real character, out-of-square openings, and forty-year-old windows losing the fight with an Indiana winter. That kind of house rewards craftsmanship over flash, and that's the work we like best.

No high-pressure pitch. No "today-only" pricing games. We look at the job, tell you plainly what it needs, and put a fair number on it. If we're not the right fit, we'll say so.
